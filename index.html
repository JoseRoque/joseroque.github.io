About

I am currently a Computer Engineering Junior at the University of Texas at Arlington.
Some of my interest include but are not limited to Optimizing Business Operations, Internet of Things, Renewable energy and Social Impact.

I have worked with many programming languages including Python, ARMv7 (Assembly), JavaScript, CSS3, HTML5, MATLAB, Java, and C. I have also come to find the intersection between hardware and software to be a rather interesting one through my work with microcontrollers such as the PIC18 or Intel Edison and Arduino Development Kits.

I feel complexity is the enemy of execution so I break problems down to their most simplest form by taking a bottom up approach and reason up from there however I also recognize the value in seeing the big picture.

I love tinkering with new technologies and frameworks and take a project-based approach to learning. Feel free to check out some of my work.
